Kanye West

Biography

K anye West,legal name Ye, born June 8, 1977, Atlanta, Georgia, United States of America. American producer, rapper, and fashion designer who parlayed his production success in the late 1990s and early 2000s into a career as a popular, critically acclaimed solo artist. West has released numerous successful albums and is recognized as a critically influential figure in the 2000s hip hop industry. His incredible sampling ability has sparked numerous producers to innovate and he has undoubtedly changed the entire sound of mainstream hip hop.
