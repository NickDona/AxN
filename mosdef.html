Mos Def

Biography

Yasiin Bey, known by his stage name Mos Def, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor. Bey began his hip hop career in 1994, alongside his siblings in the short-lived rap group Urban Thermo Dynamics, after which they appeared on albums by Da Bush Babees and De La Soul. His hop hop career has been supplemented by several acting roles in major films. His snappy flow and lyrical prowess is highly regarded by critics and fans alike. Though he is seemingly retired at this point, his contributions to artists and the music scene as a whole deserve recognition.
